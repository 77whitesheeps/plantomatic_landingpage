Choose the right planting system for your field

Different crops and fields need different planting systems. Plant-o-matic helps you understand and apply these systems correctly.

Square system

Simple to mark and manage. Plants are spaced equally in both directions, forming neat squares‚Äîideal for many vegetables and fruit trees.

Triangular system

Increases plant population without overcrowding by staggering rows, making better use of available space.

Quincunx system

Adds an extra plant in the center of each square. Useful for filler crops or temporary intercrops in orchards.
